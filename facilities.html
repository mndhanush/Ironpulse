The Iron Forge

No waiting for equipment. The Iron Forge features an expansive free-weight floor equipped with calibrated plates, Olympic platforms, and custom dumbbells up to 150 lbs.

  • Elite Free Weights

    Urethane-coated dumbbells and competition-grade kettlebells.

  • Olympic Lifting Zones

    Custom-built deadlift platforms with shock-absorbent drop zones.

  • Advanced Machines

    Biomechanically flawless plate-loaded and pin-select machines.

The Cardio Deck

Elevate your heart rate in our dedicated endurance zone. Featuring over 100 pieces of interconnected equipment, you can stream your favorite shows or track your metrics seamlessly via the IronPulse app.

  • Treadmills & Climbers

    Impact-absorbing decks and endless stair climbers for serious burn.

  • Assault & Spin Bikes

    Air resistance bikes for brutal HIIT protocols.

Functional Turf

A 50-yard indoor turf arena designed for agility, power, and athletic conditioning.

Power Sleds

Build explosive lower body strength with weighted sled pushes and pulls across the turf.

The Rig

A massive custom-built jungle gym featuring monkey bars, ring stations, and multiple pull-up grips.

Plyo & Agility

Soft plyometric boxes, battle ropes, and agility ladders to increase speed and coordination.

Recovery Suite

Training breaks you down; recovery builds you up. Our dedicated wellness suite provides the tools you need to accelerate healing and reduce muscle soreness.

  • Infrared Saunas

    Detoxify and relax stiff muscles with deep-penetrating infrared heat.

  • Cold Plunge Therapy

    Reduce inflammation instantly with our temperature-controlled ice baths.

  • Massage & Percussion

    Access to Theraguns and in-house sports massage therapists.

Premium Amenities

Finish your session in our executive locker rooms. Featuring luxury showers, complimentary towel service, grooming stations, and secure digital lockers so you can transition from the gym to the office seamlessly.

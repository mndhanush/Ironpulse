Memberships & Billing

Monthly memberships can be cancelled at any time with a 30-day written notice. You can submit this notice in person at the front desk or via the Member Dashboard in the IronPulse app. Annual and Quarterly plans require completion of their billing cycle before cancellation takes effect.

Yes! Annual and Quarterly members can freeze their membership for up to 2 consecutive months per calendar year. Medical freezes are accommodated with a doctor's note for extended periods. A small $10/month hold fee applies.

Absolutely not. IronPulse operates on 100% transparent pricing. We do not charge initiation fees, hidden annual maintenance fees, or key-fob fees. The price you see on our pricing page is the final price.

Annual Elite members receive 2 complimentary guest passes per month. All other members can purchase a day pass for a guest at a discounted rate of $15 at the front desk.

Facility & Equipment

IronPulse is accessible 24/7 for all active members via biometric entry. Our staffed hours (for tours, protein bar, and front-desk assistance) are Monday–Friday from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Saturday–Sunday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Yes. We provide complimentary sweat towels on the gym floor and premium shower towels in the locker rooms. Our digital lockers are free to use during your workout—no padlock required.

Our peak hours are typically Monday to Thursday from 5:00 PM to 7:30 PM, and mornings between 6:00 AM and 8:00 AM. You can check the live gym capacity meter in the IronPulse app before you arrive.

The Recovery Suite (saunas, cold plunges, and massage tools) is included in Annual Elite and Quarterly Pro memberships. Monthly Flex members can add Recovery Suite access for an additional $15/month.

Classes & Training

We strongly recommend booking in advance via the IronPulse App. Classes like Rhythm Spin and Power Forge frequently fill up. Booking opens 48 hours before the class start time. Walk-ins are accepted if space permits.

Yes! Our coaches are trained to provide scaling options and modifications for every movement. Let the instructor know it's your first time before class starts, and they will ensure you have a safe, effective workout.

Personal Training is purchased in session blocks (5, 10, or 20 sessions). Once purchased, you will undergo an initial assessment where we pair you with the best trainer for your specific goals (strength, hypertrophy, or weight loss). Sessions do not expire for 12 months.
